About Me

Hey, my name's Andrew. I have an MA in History from GMU and taught history at the high school and (community) college level for five years. I have spent three of those years in Special Education helping students with special needs succeed. I have accumulated a lot of patience from painting tiny men and teaching students of various spectrums/backgrounds, so coding has at least been a very fun (and safe) kind of suffering!

I really enjoy history, especially military history, and spend too much time playing city simulators and wargames. Speaking of wargames, I do those analog--I also like painting miniatures and do commission work when I have time. As you can see I have literally zero background in this computer stuff. I have roughly zero talent and everything takes me a lot of practice to learn. That said, learning has been rewarding and it feels like it's just a matter of practice. I really enjoy playing around with code to learn how it works--it 'feels real' when you go and mess up someone else's code and learn how it all works.
